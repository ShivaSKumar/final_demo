WHAT IS REVIEW SUBMISSION PORTAL?

Welcome to the review submission platform for all editors and Journalists at GameRanks. This is the companiesâ€™ online portal to submit any and all reviews for submission to your supervising editors for review prior to publication on our official website for our audiences to consume. When adding a review please, indicate your name, game title, the review itself and the final score (should be an integer scale from 1 to 10, where 1 is poor and 10 is a masterpiece) for said game review. Additionally, you can view all pending reviews for editing from your fellow colleagues. Feel free to edit them if you feel some points were missed but be advised any edits made to the reviews must be legitimate critiques and should not be made to undermine your colleagues work.
